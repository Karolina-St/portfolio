## Context

Investment teams spent hours assembling bespoke report packages. Clients struggled to find historical comparisons and missed updates when files lived in inboxes.

## Approach

Information architecture was modeled around the questions clients actually ask each quarter. We introduced saved views, audit-friendly access logs, and export options that matched compliance expectations.

## Outcomes

- Fewer ad-hoc data requests post-launch
- Stronger trust signals through clear sourcing and timestamps
- Foundation for self-serve exploration of approved metrics
