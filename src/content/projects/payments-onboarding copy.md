## Context

Drop-off spiked at the step where users connected external accounts and verified identity. Research showed confusion about why each step mattered and how long the process would take.

## Approach

We reframed the flow as a transparent checklist with time estimates and plain-language explanations tied to regulatory requirements. Visual density was reduced; errors were rewritten with recovery paths.

## Outcomes

- Higher completion at the verification stage in usability tests
- Support tickets for “stuck” onboarding decreased after launch
- Patterns reused in adjacent money-movement flows
