## Context

The design team doubled in a year. Critique formats varied by squad, and handoff quality was uneven.

## Approach

We introduced a shared checklist for “definition of ready” before dev pickup, a rotating facilitation guide for critique, and a living pattern index linked to the component library.

## Outcomes

- Shorter review cycles and fewer regressions in visual QA
- New hires could onboard to ways of working within their first sprint
